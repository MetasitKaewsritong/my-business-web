Our Services

NoBugs offers a comprehensive range of pest management services designed to meet the specific needs of residential and commercial clients. We provide everything from immediate infestation removal to long-term prevention.

Residential Pest Control

We protect your home and family from unwanted intruders, ensuring a safe and comfortable living space.

  • General Pest Management: Year-round plans targeting ants, spiders, silverfish, earwigs, and other common pests.
  • Termite Inspection & Treatment: Advanced detection and tailored treatments, including baiting systems and liquid barriers.
  • Rodent Exclusion & Removal: Humane traps, removal, sealing entry points, and sanitation guidance.
  • Wasp & Bee Nest Removal: Safe removal of stinging insect nests from eaves, walls, and outdoor spaces.
  • Bed Bug Elimination: Heat and chemical treatments to eradicate all life stages of bed bugs.

Commercial Pest Control

Customized solutions that help businesses comply with health regulations and protect their reputation and inventory.

  • Integrated Pest Management (IPM): Prevention-focused, low-pesticide approach ideal for restaurants, hospitals, schools, and offices.
  • Food Service Pest Control: Specialized treatment for kitchens and dining areas, with required documentation for inspections.
  • Industrial & Warehouse Management: Large-scale pest defense and stored-product pest control for industrial locations.
  • Fumigation Services: For severe infestations where standard treatments are insufficient.

Specialty & Emergency Services

Advanced solutions for urgent or unique pest challenges.

  • Wildlife Exclusion: Humane trapping, relocation, and sealing access points for animals like raccoons or squirrels.
  • Moisture Control: Reducing humidity and moisture sources that attract termites and wood-boring insects.
  • Emergency Response: Rapid deployment for severe infestations or hazardous pest incidents.
  • Pest Control Consulting: Expert reports and guidance for real estate inspections and property managers.
